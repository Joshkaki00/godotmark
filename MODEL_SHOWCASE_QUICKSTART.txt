================================================================================
TROUBLESHOOTING
================================================================================

Audio not playing:
  - Check that art/model-test/Excelsior In Aeternum.ogg exists
  - Verify audio import settings

HDR not loading:
  - Check that art/model-test/sunflowers_puresky_2k.hdr exists
  - Re-import in Godot Editor

Low FPS:
  - Press Q to lower quality preset
  - Check thermal throttling (press V for temps)
  - Verify GPU drivers are up to date

Particles not visible:
  - Wait until Phase 4 (36 seconds)
  - Check quality is Medium or higher
  - Enable verbose logging (V key)
